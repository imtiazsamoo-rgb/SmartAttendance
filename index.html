Camera Access Denied

Why is this happening?

Google Apps Script blocks camera access by default when running via the standard /exec link because it runs inside a restricted iframe.

How to fix this:
  1. Click the Open Camera Mode button below to open the app directly without the Google wrapper.
  2. If it still fails, check the Lock icon (🔒) in your address bar and allow the camera.
  3. Check your Windows Camera Privacy Settings to ensure "Allow desktop apps to access your camera" is ON.
